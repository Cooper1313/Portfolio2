Community Wealth & Student Opportunity Research

Research paper analyzes the impact of rapid population growth and uneven infrastructure development on public schools in North Florida. Focusing on Duval and St. Johns Counties, it explores how overcrowding, housing pressure, and environmental vulnerability intersect to affect educational access, teacher capacity, and community resilience. The study highlights structural imbalances and proposes frameworks for sustainable, equity-driven policy reform.
